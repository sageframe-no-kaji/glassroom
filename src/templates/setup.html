Welcome to Glassroom

Let's get you set up. This takes about 2 minutes.

1. Log in
2. Select classes
3. First scrape
4. Download PDFs

Step 1 — Log in to Google Classroom

We'll open a browser window. Sign in to your Google account that has access to your child's Google Classroom. Once logged in, close or leave that window — we'll detect the login automatically.

Step 2 — Select classes to track

Choose which classes you want to follow. You can change this later.

Step 3 — Scrape assignments

We'll open the Google Classroom pages for each selected class and collect assignments. This usually takes 1–3 minutes. The browser will be visible so you can see it working.

Step 4 — Download assignment PDFs

Optional: download PDF attachments for all assignments so you have local copies.
